Musical Landscapes

Eclectic, sensitive selections off the beaten track: ambient, jazz, hard-lounge, soft rock, pop, soul & neo-soul, real hip-hop, disco, funk, New York house, Detroit techno, Afro-Brazilian rhythms, groove rarities, timeless hits and gems of all kinds.

Every set is a story, a sonic journey that respects the moment, its temporality, and its own energy. No Top 50 of contemporary commercial ugliness, but iconic, known, recognized, forgotten, or unknown tracks, selected for their quality and power. Between class and warmth, between dance and contemplation: a musical aesthetic designed to touch both body and mind, from the cocktail hour to the afterparty.

With nearly 15 years of life abroad, including 10 years in Berlin, my multicultural vibe has developed, and it will therefore feel completely natural to perform in such a context.

A music lover from a young age, I started spinning records at Radio Campus in Orléans when I was 18, then moved on to my first dancing bar experiences in Annecy during my studies, at a legendary spot that is now closed.

Raised by the French Touch (Daft Punk, Cassius, etc...), the sirens of Berlin's "underground" scene called strongly, even leading me to step into club doors to play records. Radios, bars, clubs, festivals, pajama parties, banquets, I adapt while keeping a consistent and adventurous thread of quality.

For weddings, an immersion into musical comfort zones for everyone’s enjoyment, followed by deeper waters depending on the vibe of the night!

Musical performance with or without sound/light setup. Vinyl Bar option available. Vinyl & digital setup. Based in Lot (46), travel possible.
